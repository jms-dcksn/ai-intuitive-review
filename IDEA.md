# AI Intuitive Review


This project explores human validation of LLM and AI Agent outputs. 

We'll explore Agentic UX and the different UX features needed to build trust and confidence with AI systems that make recommendations, deliver analyses, and potentially take action with approval. 

This is key to AI in the enterprise - how do we progressively build trust with users?

## Topics

- Citations
- Progressive disclosure to humans (links, expandable cards etc.)
- iterative walkthroughs of agentic responses
- reasoning tokens as displayable proof
- Attribution of findings in large documents through clickable navigation and highlighting
